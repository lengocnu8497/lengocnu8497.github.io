CPP Parking Solution

OUR IDEA

Our goal is to create a mobile app where we can help college students reduce the time it takes to find parking on and off campus.

  • An app that solves the parking complications on campus
  • Keeps track of how many parking spots are available in each parking lot on and off campus that students/staff have access to
  • User can spend less time looking for a spot since they will already know where the open spots are located

OUR PROCESS



Sketched out a low fidelity mockup I and the team had a initial meet up where we had a discussion about the goal of this project and how we were going to solve it through creating a user-centric layout.





Our first version of high fidelity mockup.











Our second version of high fidelity mockup. After a round of user survey with 10 participants, collected data suggested that our design was too bulky and the color was not pleasant on the eye. As a result, we decided to redesign the whole layout with softer look and better color scheme so that it is easy on the eye of the users.
